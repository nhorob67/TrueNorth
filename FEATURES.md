# TrueNorth Features

## Strategy

### Launch
Onboarding wizard for new ventures. Guides teams through strategic setup including venture name, BHAG (Big Hairy Audacious Goal), strategic filters, and annual outcomes. Supports first-venture and second-venture flows with context from other ventures.

### Cockpit
Executive dashboard showing at-a-glance operational status. Displays drifting KPIs (red/yellow status), open decisions, at-risk bets, open blockers, upcoming moves due within 7 days, pending commitments, today's pulses, stalled bets detection, operating health score with trend indicators, cadence report, and AI recommendations from the Signal Watch agent.

### Portfolio
Multi-venture overview page (only for orgs with multiple ventures). Shows aggregate metrics per venture including active bets count, bet health distribution, red/yellow KPIs, blockers, pulse count, and team members. Also shows cross-venture roll-up of open blockers, commitments, decisions, and team pulses for the current day.

### Vision
Vision board for long-term strategic direction. Stores and displays vision documents for the year with historical snapshots showing how the vision has evolved.

### Scoreboard
KPI tracking dashboard. Displays all active KPIs organized by tier with their current values, targets, units, and historical entry data for trend analysis.

## Execution

### Bets
Bets war room showing all active bets with their outcome statements and associated moves (milestones and recurring rhythms). Central place to manage strategic bets and track progress toward outcomes.

### Ideas
Idea vault and backlog system. Shows all submitted ideas with lifecycle stages (submitted, candidate, selected, approved, rejected). Ideas are evaluated against strategic filters and scored against a configurable threshold (default 70) for progression to candidate stage. Supports a 14-day quarantine period before ideas can move through the pipeline.

### Content
Content management system. Displays content pieces organized by creation date, enabling teams to manage content strategy as it relates to bets and funnel initiatives.

### Funnels
Funnel registry tracking customer acquisition and conversion funnels. Allows linking approved ideas to funnels and monitoring funnel metrics and health.

## Operations

### Pulse
Weekly team health check-in system. Shows the current user's pulse (if submitted today), team pulses with avatars, active bets for reference, user's pulse streak, personal todos sidebar, and recurring move rhythms with instance completion tracking by cycle.

### Sync
Weekly sync meeting prep and follow-up. Aggregates red/yellow KPIs with action playbooks, active bets with move details, open blockers (sorted by age/severity with linked entities), open decisions awaiting input, open issues (open/investigating status), and last week's commitments. Includes team roster for assignment.

### Operations
Operations tracker displaying recent decisions (not yet decided), blockers (all states), commitments (all states), and issues (all states) for operational management and visibility.

### Processes
Process library and workflow management. Lists all processes with lifecycle status (draft, active, retired) and owner information. Central place to document and maintain operational processes.

## Insights

### Health
Organizational operating health dashboard. Computes a composite health score and status based on multiple metrics. Shows recent historical snapshots with trend charts, AI-generated interpretation of health trends, and per-venture views for multi-venture orgs.

### Narratives
AI-powered narrative generation tool. Creates automated narrative reports about venture performance using various templates (executive summary, deep-dive analysis, etc.) with configurable time windows. Maintains a history of generated narratives for reference.

## Meta

### Artifacts
Artifact staleness tracker. Monitors documents, visions, and other living documents to ensure they stay current. Flags which artifacts are stale (haven't been updated within their configured threshold) to maintain quality and relevance.

### Activity
Activity feed of all comments and discussions across the platform. Shows comments from all entities (bets, ideas, KPIs, etc.) with author names, timestamps, resolved/unresolved status, and mentions.

### Todos
Personal task management. Shows the user's todo list organized by completion status, priority, and due date. Todos can be linked to any entity (bet, idea, KPI, etc.) in the system for context.

## Account

### Settings (Admin only)
Admin panel for organization configuration, agents setup, policies, automation, and cron job management.

### Profile
User profile page showing role card (capabilities/scope), owned KPIs and bets, active commitments, and pulse streak.
